<p><p>AC Ride on Floor Scrubbers refer to floor cleaning machines that are powered by alternating current (AC). These machines are typically connected to an external power source and offer higher runtime and cleaning efficiency. On the other hand, DC Ride on Floor Scrubbers are powered by direct current (DC) and generally utilize batteries for operation. These machines provide greater mobility and are suitable for areas with limited access to power outlets. Both types serve the same purpose of efficiently cleaning large floor areas, but differ in terms of power source and operational characteristics.</p></p>
