<p><ul><li>Prick</li><li>Intradermal</li><li>Patch</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Allergy Skin Test market includes three main types: Prick, Intradermal, and Patch tests. Prick tests involve putting small drops of allergen extracts on the skin and then gently pricking the skin's surface to allow the allergen to enter. Intradermal tests involve injecting a small amount of allergen under the skin's surface with a fine needle. Patch tests involve applying allergens to adhesive patches and then placing them on the skin for a specific duration. These tests help identify allergens causing allergic reactions and aid in developing suitable treatment plans.</p></p>
