<p><p>Floating hotels can be classified into two main market types: moveable and fixed. Moveable floating hotels are designed to travel to different locations, allowing passengers to experience various destinations during their stay. They can be compared to cruise ships, offering flexibility and exploration. On the other hand, fixed floating hotels remain stationary at a specific location, such as a river or a coastal area. These hotels provide a unique experience of staying on the water, with picturesque views and a peaceful atmosphere.</p></p>
