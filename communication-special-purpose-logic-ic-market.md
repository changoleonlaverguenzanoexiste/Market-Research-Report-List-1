<p><p>The Communication Special Purpose Logic IC market includes various types of logic integrated circuits (ICs) used in communication systems. TTL IC (Transistor-transistor Logic) utilizes bipolar transistors for high-speed operation. CMOS Logic IC (Complementary Metal Oxide Semiconductor) consumes low power and offers high noise immunity. BiCMOS Logic IC (Bipolar CMOS) combines attributes of both TTL and CMOS, providing high-speed operation with low power consumption. These ICs are crucial components in communication devices, enabling efficient data processing and transmission.</p></p>
