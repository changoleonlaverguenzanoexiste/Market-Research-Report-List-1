<p><p>The riveting tools market is segmented into three types: pneumatic riveting tools, battery-operated riveting tools, and hand-operated riveting tools. Pneumatic riveting tools are powered by compressed air and are often used in industrial settings due to their high power and efficiency. Battery-operated riveting tools are portable and do not require a power source, making them suitable for remote or outdoor applications. Hand-operated riveting tools, as the name suggests, are manually operated and commonly used in small-scale projects or where power sources are not available.</p></p>
