<p><p>The petrol grass cutters market includes two main types: self-propelled and push drive. Self-propelled grass cutters are equipped with a motor that powers the machine, enabling it to move without the need for physical force from the operator. This type offers greater ease and less physical effort in cutting grass. On the other hand, push drive grass cutters require the operator to push and maneuver the machine manually, using their own physical strength to operate the cutter. While they require more effort from the operator, push drive cutters are typically more affordable compared to self-propelled ones.</p></p>
