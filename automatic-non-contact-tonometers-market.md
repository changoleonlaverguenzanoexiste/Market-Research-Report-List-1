<p><p>Automatic Non-Contact Tonometers are medical devices used to measure the intraocular pressure of the eye without making direct contact. They are commonly used in hospitals, eye clinics, and optical stores.</p><p>The Automatic Non-Contact Tonometers Market is expected to witness steady growth during the forecast period.
