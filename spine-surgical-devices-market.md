<p><ul><li>Spinal Fusion Products</li><li>Non-fusion Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The spine surgical devices market consists of two main types: spinal fusion products and non-fusion products. Spinal fusion products are used to join or stabilize two or more vertebrae, promoting bone growth and restricting movement. Non-fusion products aim to preserve spinal motion while providing support and stability. These devices include artificial discs, dynamic stabilization devices, and artificial facet joints. Spinal fusion is the more traditional and widely used method, whereas non-fusion techniques are gaining popularity due to their potential to maintain natural spine movement and potentially reduce complications.</p></p>
