Common mode noise refers to any unwanted or interfering electrical signals that appear in both the signal and ground wires of a circuit. These inductors work by creating a magnetic field that opposes the common mode noise and thus reduces its impact on the circuit.</p><p>The common mode inductors market is expected to experience steady growth in the coming years. SMD Common Mode Choke Coils are compact and efficient, suitable for modern electronic devices like smartphones and tablets. They are designed to minimize electromagnetic interference, providing a noise-free signal transmission. On the other hand, Through Hole Common Mode Choke Coils are larger in size and are commonly used in industrial and high-power applications. They offer higher power handling capabilities and are ideal for filtering common mode noise in power circuits.</p></p>
