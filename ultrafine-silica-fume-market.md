<p><p>Ultrafine silica fume is categorized into three main types based on its density: densified silica fume, semi-densified silica fume, and undensified silica fume. Densified silica fume undergoes a process to increase its density, making it easier to handle and transport. Semi-densified silica fume is partially compacted, providing a balance between handling convenience and performance. Undensified silica fume, on the other hand, is in its raw form and requires additional processing before use. Each type caters to different requirements in various industries.</p></p>
