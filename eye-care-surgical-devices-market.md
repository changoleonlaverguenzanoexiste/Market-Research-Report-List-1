<p><strong>The Eye Care Surgical Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Detection Devices</li><li>Surgical Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Eye care surgical devices refer to various equipment and instruments used during eye surgeries to aid in detection and the surgical process itself. Detection devices are used to diagnose various eye conditions such as cataracts, glaucoma, and retinal disorders. These devices include fundus cameras, optical coherence tomography systems, and visual field analyzers. Surgical devices, on the other hand, are employed during specific eye surgeries, such as LASIK, cataract extraction, and vitrectomy. These devices may include microkeratomes, intraocular lenses, and vitrectomy probes, among others. Together, these devices play a crucial role in improving eye care outcomes and patient vision.</p></p>
