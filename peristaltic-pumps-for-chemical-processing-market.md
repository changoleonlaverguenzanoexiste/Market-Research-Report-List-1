<p><ul><li>Roller Peristaltic Pump</li><li>Shoe type Peristaltic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Peristaltic pumps for chemical processing come in two main types: roller peristaltic pumps and shoe type peristaltic pumps. Roller peristaltic pumps use rotating rollers to squeeze the tubing, creating pressure to move the chemical. Shoe type peristaltic pumps have a flexible shoe that presses against the tubing to create the necessary pressure. Both types are commonly used in the chemical processing industry due to their ability to handle abrasive and viscous fluids, as well as their ease of maintenance and precise flow control capabilities.</p></p>
