<p><ul><li>Single Oils</li><li>Blends Oils</li><li>Carrier Oils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aromatherapy essential oils market consists of three main types: single oils, blend oils, and carrier oils. Single oils are extracted from a single plant, such as lavender or peppermint, and are used for specific therapeutic purposes. Blend oils are a combination of multiple single oils, designed to provide a more holistic approach to aromatherapy. Lastly, carrier oils are used to dilute and enhance the effectiveness of the essential oils. Each type plays a unique role in the aromatherapy market, catering to different needs and preferences of consumers.</p></p>
