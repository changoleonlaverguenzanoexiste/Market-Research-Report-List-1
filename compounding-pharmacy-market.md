CUPM refers to Complementary & Alternative Medicines Pharmacy, which focuses on providing alternative treatment options. PDA refers to Professional Dispensing of Allopathic Medicines, involving the dispensing of conventional medications. SAPM stands for Specialized Admixtures for Parenteral Medicines, highlighting pharmacies specializing in creating injectable medications. These market types cater to specific needs and preferences within the compounding pharmacy industry.</p></p>
