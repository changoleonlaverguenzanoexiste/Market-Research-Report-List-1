<p><p>The food flow packing machine market is divided into two types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal machines are designed to pack products that are laid horizontally on the conveyor belt, such as bread, cookies, and chocolates. These machines typically have a higher speed and are suitable for large scale production. On the other hand, vertical machines are used to package products that are standing vertically, such as bottles, cans, and pouches. They are more versatile and customizable to various product shapes and sizes.</p></p>
