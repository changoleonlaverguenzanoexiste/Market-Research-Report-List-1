<p><p>Retail ERP software can be categorized into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise ERP solutions require the software to be installed on the company's own servers and computers. This type of software offers a higher degree of control and customization but requires more investment in hardware and maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based ERP software is hosted on remote servers and can be accessed through an internet connection. This type provides flexibility, scalability, and reduced upfront costs, making it more attractive for small and medium-sized retailers.</p></p>
