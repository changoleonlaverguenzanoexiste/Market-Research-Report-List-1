<p><p>Semiconductor tapes refer to specialized adhesive tapes used in the semiconductor manufacturing process. Back grinding tapes are designed to hold and protect delicate semiconductor wafers during the back grinding process, which involves thinning the wafer. These tapes offer high adhesion and excellent heat resistance. Dicing tapes are used to securely fix the thinned wafers during the dicing process, where the wafers are cut into individual chips. These tapes provide a strong bonding force and ensure precise cutting, while also facilitating easy chip removal after dicing.</p></p>
