<p><p>The New Energy ERP market consists of two main types: On-Premise ERP and Cloud-based ERP. On-Premise ERP refers to software that is installed and operated on the company's own servers and infrastructure. This type of ERP offers more control and data security, but requires higher upfront investment and maintenance costs. On the other hand, Cloud-based ERP is hosted on a remote server and accessed through the internet. It offers greater flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, but may raise concerns about data privacy and reliance on internet connectivity.</p></p>
