<p><strong>The Mining Thickeners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Peripheral Drive</li><li>Center Drive</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Mining thickeners are used in the mining industry to separate solids from liquids. There are two types of mining thickeners: peripheral drive and center drive. </p><p>Peripheral drive thickeners have a unique structure where the feed enters through a feedwell and is spread evenly across the tank. This allows for efficient sedimentation and thickening of the solids. </p><p>On the other hand, center drive thickeners have the feed entering through a central tube, causing the solids to settle at the bottom. This design allows for a higher concentration of solids in the thickened slurry. Both types of thickeners have their own advantages and are suitable for different mining operations.</p></p>
