<p><p>A nasal suction pump is a medical device that is used to remove mucus and secretions from the nasal passages. It is commonly used in infants and young children who are unable to blow their own noses. The device consists of a tip that is inserted into the nostril and a bulb or chamber that is used to create suction, thereby helping to clear the nasal passages and improve breathing.</p><p>The current outlook of the nasal suction pump market is promising, with steady growth expected in the coming years. The rising prevalence of respiratory disorders, such as allergies and infections, among both children and adults is a major factor driving the demand for nasal suction pumps.
