<p><p>Telecom obstruction lighting systems market types refer to the different levels of intensity used to ensure safety and visibility of telecommunication towers and tall structures, such as buildings, wind turbines, and antennas. Low intensity systems emit a steady red light, suitable for structures under 150 feet. Medium intensity systems use flashing or steady red lights, typically for structures between 150-500 feet. High intensity systems employ flashing white lights, crucial for structures exceeding 500 feet. These different intensity levels are designed to comply with aviation regulations and minimize the risk of collisions with aircraft.</p></p>
