<p><p>Hydraulic mining shovels are used in both surface and sub-surface mining operations. They are used to efficiently remove large volumes of overburden and load the extracted material onto trucks for transportation. In sub-surface mining, hydraulic mining shovels are utilized to excavate minerals and materials from underground mines, enabling access to valuable resources like gold, diamonds, and silver. These shovels are crucial for efficient and productive mining operations in both surface and sub-surface environments.</p></p>
