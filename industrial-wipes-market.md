<p><p>The industrial wipes market consists of different types, including cellulose, poly/cellulose blend, and woodpulp/polyester. Cellulose wipes are made from natural fibers and are highly absorbent, suitable for cleaning surfaces or equipment in various industries. Poly/cellulose blend wipes combine the absorbent properties of cellulose with the strength of synthetic fibers, making them durable and effective for heavy-duty cleaning tasks. Woodpulp/polyester wipes are a combination of woodpulp and polyester fibers, offering excellent absorbency and strength. These wipes cater to different industrial needs, providing efficient cleaning solutions.</p></p>
