<p><strong>The Mask Inspection Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Die to Die (DD) Method</li><li>Die to Database (DB) Method</li></ul></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Mask Inspection Machine Market Types refer to different methods used for inspecting masks in the semiconductor industry. The Die to Die (DD) Method involves comparing patterns of adjacent dies on a mask to identify defects. In contrast, the Die to Database (DB) Method compares the patterns on the mask to a reference database of the expected design. Both methods aim to detect defects, but the DD method is more suitable for random defects, while the DB method is better for systematic defects. These market types offer different approaches to mask inspection based on the specific needs of semiconductor manufacturers.</p></p>
