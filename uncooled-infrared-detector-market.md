<p><strong>The Uncooled Infrared Detector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bolometer</li><li>Pyroelectric Detector</li><li>Thermopile Detector</li><li>Diode Detector</li><li>Thermal Capacitance Detector</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The uncooled infrared detector market comprises several types of detectors. The bolometer is a widely used type that measures the change in resistance caused by infrared radiation. Pyroelectric detectors generate voltage when their temperature changes due to radiation. Thermopile detectors consist of multiple thermocouples that generate a voltage proportional to the incident radiation. Diode detectors convert infrared radiation into an electrical signal. Thermal capacitance detectors use the change in heat capacity to measure infrared radiation. Lastly, there are other detectors that utilize different mechanisms to measure infrared radiation.</p></p>
