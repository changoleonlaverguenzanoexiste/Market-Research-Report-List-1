<p><p>The concrete inspection system market consists of various types, namely ultrasonic inspection and CT (Computed Tomography) inspection. Ultrasonic inspection involves the use of sound waves to detect and evaluate any defects, cracks, or voids in concrete structures. On the other hand, CT inspection utilizes X-ray technology to create detailed images of the internal structure of concrete, providing accurate information about its composition and structural integrity. Both methods play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and safety of concrete structures by identifying any potential issues and guiding appropriate repairs or maintenance measures.</p></p>
