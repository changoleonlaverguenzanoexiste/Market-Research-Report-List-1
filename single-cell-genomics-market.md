<p><p>The single cell genomics market consists of three main types: cell isolation, sample preparation, and genomic analysis. Cell isolation involves the separation and collection of individual cells from a larger population. Sample preparation involves the processing and purification of these isolated cells, ensuring their suitability for subsequent analysis. Genomic analysis refers to the study of the genetic material within the isolated cells, enabling researchers to understand gene expression, mutations, and other genomic features at a single-cell level. Each of these market segments plays a crucial role in advancing the field of single cell genomics.</p></p>
