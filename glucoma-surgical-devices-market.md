<p><p>The glaucoma surgical devices market can be categorized into three main types: Glaucoma Drainage Devices, Glaucoma Laser Devices, and Implants & Stents. Glaucoma Drainage Devices are used to regulate intraocular pressure by creating a new drainage pathway for excess fluid. Glaucoma Laser Devices utilize laser technology to improve fluid outflow and reduce eye pressure. Implants & Stents are small devices implanted in the eye to improve fluid drainage and reduce intraocular pressure, offering a minimally invasive alternative to traditional glaucoma surgeries.</p></p>
