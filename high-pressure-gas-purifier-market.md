<p><p>The high-pressure gas purifier market includes three types: single column purifier, double column purifier, and multi-column purifier. A single column purifier removes impurities from gas through a single purification process. A double column purifier consists of two columns which allows for a more efficient purification process. On the other hand, a multi-column purifier includes multiple columns, enabling simultaneous purification of different gases. These different types cater to varying needs of industries requiring high-pressure gas purification, offering solutions that range from simple to more complex purification processes.</p></p>
