<p><p>Brake fluid is a vital component of a vehicle's braking system as it transfers force applied to the brake pedal to the brake pads or shoes. The market offers different types of brake fluids labeled as DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 and DOT 4 are glycol-based fluids suitable for most vehicles, with DOT 4 offering higher boiling points for extreme conditions. DOT 5 is silicone-based and not compatible with glycol-based fluids, typically used in older vehicles or those requiring specific properties. DOT 5.1 is a glycol-based fluid with enhanced performance characteristics for high-performance vehicles.</p></p>
