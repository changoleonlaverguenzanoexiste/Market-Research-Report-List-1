<p><strong>The Carbon Nanotube (CNT) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SWCNTs</li><li>MWCNTs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon Nanotubes (CNTs) can be categorized into two main types - Single-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (SWCNTs) and Multi-Walled Carbon Nanotubes (MWCNTs). SWCNTs consist of a single layer of carbon atoms arranged in a cylindrical structure, while MWCNTs consist of multiple layers of carbon atoms arranged concentrically. SWCNTs are known for their exceptional electrical and thermal conductivity, whereas MWCNTs are valued for their mechanical strength and structural integrity. Both types of CNTs have diverse applications in areas such as electronics, materials science, and biotechnology.</p></p>
