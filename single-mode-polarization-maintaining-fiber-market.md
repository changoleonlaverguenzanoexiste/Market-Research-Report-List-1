<p><p>The single mode polarization maintaining fiber market offers different types of fibers, such as Panda Fiber, Bow Tie Fiber, Elliptical Fiber, and Others (including Photonic Crystal Fibers). Panda Fiber maintains polarization by manipulating the stress-induced birefringence in the fiber core. Bow Tie Fiber achieves polarization maintenance by using a bow-tie-shaped fiber core. Elliptical Fiber has an elliptical-shaped core to maintain polarization. Photonic Crystal Fibers have a unique structure that creates a highly controlled waveguide, resulting in excellent polarization maintaining properties. These fiber types cater to various applications and market demands.</p></p>
