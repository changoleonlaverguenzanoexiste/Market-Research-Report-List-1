<p><ul><li>Sublingual Film</li><li>Fast Dissolving Dental/Buccal Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Oral thin films are a type of drug delivery system that dissolves quickly in the mouth, allowing for easy consumption without the need for water. There are two main types of oral thin films: sublingual films and fast-dissolving dental/buccal films. Sublingual films are placed under the tongue and dissolve, allowing for rapid absorption into the bloodstream. Fast-dissolving dental/buccal films are applied directly to the gums or cheek and also dissolve quickly, releasing the medication into the oral cavity for absorption.</p></p>
