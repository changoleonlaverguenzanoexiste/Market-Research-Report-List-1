<p><p>A derinder machine, also known as a meat derinder, is a specialized equipment used in the food processing industry to remove the skin and fat from meat products such as pork, beef, and poultry. It improves efficiency and reduces manual labor required for the derinding process.</p><p>The derinder machine market is witnessing steady growth due to the increasing demand for processed meat products.
