<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Stacked CMOS sensor market includes two types: 2-layer Stacked and 3-layer Stacked sensors. In the case of 2-layer Stacked sensors, the image sensor and signal processing circuitry are stacked in two separate layers, allowing for improved image quality and faster data processing. On the other hand, 3-layer Stacked sensors have an additional layer of DRAM (dynamic random access memory) integrated into the stack of the image sensor and circuitry layers. This added DRAM layer enables high-speed data transfer, leading to enhanced overall performance and improved image capture capabilities.</p></p>
