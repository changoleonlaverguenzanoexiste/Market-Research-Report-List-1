<p><p>An Automated Test System (ATS) is a software-driven platform designed to automate the testing process of various products or systems. The increasing complexity of products, such as electronic devices or software applications, necessitates the use of automated testing systems to identify defects, performance issues, and compatibility problems early in the development phase.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing emphasis on reducing testing time and costs, along with the need for continuous testing and deployment models, has propelled the adoption of Agile and DevOps methodologies.
