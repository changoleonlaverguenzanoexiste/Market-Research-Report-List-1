<p><strong>The Tapered Implant Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Root-form Dental Implants</li><li>Plate-form Dental Implants</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tapered implant market consists of two main types: root-form dental implants and plate-form dental implants. Root-form dental implants are designed with a shape similar to a tooth root and provide stability and support to the artificial tooth. Plate-form dental implants, on the other hand, feature a flat plate design which is advantageous for individuals with limited jawbone width. Both types of implants serve as effective solutions for replacing missing teeth, offering durability and a natural appearance to patients.</p></p>
