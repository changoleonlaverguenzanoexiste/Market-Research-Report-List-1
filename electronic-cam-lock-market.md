<p><p>The Electronic Cam Lock market includes two main types: keypad lock and digital lock. Keypad locks require users to enter a numeric code on a keypad interface to unlock the lock. On the other hand, digital locks use electronic signals to authenticate users, often through biometric features like fingerprints or facial recognition. They provide enhanced security by requiring specific user identification. Both types of locks are commonly used in various applications, including cabinets, lockers, and secure storage systems.</p></p>
