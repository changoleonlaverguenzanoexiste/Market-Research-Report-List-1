<p><p>The fruit and vegetable washing machine market offers various types of machines, including ultrasonic and ozone types. Ultrasonic machines use sound waves to create microscopic bubbles that clean the produce through cavitation. They are effective in removing dirt, pesticides, and bacteria. On the other hand, ozone machines use ozone gas to disinfect fruits and vegetables by destroying harmful microorganisms. Ozone is a powerful oxidizing agent that can kill bacteria, viruses, and fungi. Both types of machines provide efficient and hygienic cleaning solutions for consumers.</p></p>
