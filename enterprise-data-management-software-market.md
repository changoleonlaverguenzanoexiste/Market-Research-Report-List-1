<p><p>Enterprise Data Management software refers to solutions that help organizations effectively store, analyze, process, and manage their data. The market for such software can be divided into two types: on-premise and hosted. On-premise software is installed and operated locally on the organization's own servers, providing full control over data management. Hosted software, on the other hand, is cloud-based and managed by a third-party vendor. This allows organizations to outsource their data management needs, reducing infrastructure costs and providing flexibility to access data from anywhere with an internet connection.</p></p>
