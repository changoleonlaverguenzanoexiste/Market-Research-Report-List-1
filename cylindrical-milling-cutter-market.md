<p><ul><li>Straight Teeth</li><li>Spiral Teeth</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cylindrical milling cutter market can be divided into two types: straight teeth and spiral teeth. Straight teeth milling cutters have teeth that are arranged in a straight line along the circumference of the cutter. They are suitable for performing heavy-duty milling tasks and are commonly used in milling machines. On the other hand, spiral teeth milling cutters have teeth that are arranged in a helical pattern, which helps in reducing vibration and providing a smoother cutting action. They are suitable for high-speed cutting and are commonly used in CNC machines.</p></p>
