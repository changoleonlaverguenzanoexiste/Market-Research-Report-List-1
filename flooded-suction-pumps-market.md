<p><p>Flooded suction pumps are classified into two main types: horizontal pumps and vertical pumps. Horizontal pumps have a horizontal shaft and are typically used for applications where space is limited or where the pump needs to be installed at ground level. Vertical pumps, on the other hand, have a vertical shaft and are commonly used in applications where the pump needs to be submerged in the fluid being pumped. Both types of pumps are designed to handle fluids with high solid content and are widely used in various industries for efficient pumping operations.</p></p>
