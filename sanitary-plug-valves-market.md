<p><p>Sanitary plug valves are commonly used in the food, beverage, and pharmaceutical industries for controlling the flow of fluids. The market includes two types of plug valves: sanitary 2-way plug valves and sanitary 3-way plug valves. </p><p>A sanitary 2-way plug valve has two openings, allowing fluids to flow in two directions. This type is useful for diverting or shutting off the flow of liquids and is commonly employed in applications that require simple on/off control.</p><p>On the other hand, a sanitary 3-way plug valve has three openings, providing more versatility in fluid management. It allows for the diversion of flow between multiple directions, enabling efficient control of mixing, blending, or diverting operations.</p><p>Both types of sanitary plug valves provide a hygienic solution with easy maintenance, making them indispensable in industries with stringent sanitation requirements.</p></p>
