<p><strong>The Intraosseous Infusion Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Battery Powered Driver</li><li>Manual IO Needles</li><li>Impact Driven Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intraosseous infusion device market includes various types such as battery-powered drivers, manual IO needles, and impact-driven devices. Battery-powered drivers are devices that use battery power to drive the needle into the bone for infusion. Manual IO needles are manually inserted into the bone to establish access for infusion. Impact-driven devices operate by providing a sudden force to the needle, allowing it to penetrate the bone. These different types cater to the diverse needs of healthcare professionals in situations where intravenous access may be difficult or impossible.</p></p>
