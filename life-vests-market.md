<p><p>Life vests can be categorized into three main types - foam vests, inflatable vests, and hybrid vests. Foam vests are made from buoyant materials such as foam, which provides inherent buoyancy and ensures flotation without requiring any activation. Inflatable vests, on the other hand, are designed to be manually or automatically inflated with gas chambers or CO2 cartridges. Hybrid vests combine both foam and inflatable elements, offering the advantages of both types. These different types cater to the varying needs and preferences of individuals in terms of comfort, convenience, and safety.</p></p>
