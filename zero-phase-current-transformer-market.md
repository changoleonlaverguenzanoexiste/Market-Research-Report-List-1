<p><p>In the Zero Phase Current Transformer (ZCT) market, there are two main types: Split Type and Through Type. Split Type ZCTs consist of two separate sections that can be split for easy installation, making them suitable for retrofit applications. They have a high accuracy and are used in low-voltage systems. On the other hand, Through Type ZCTs have a compact design and offer excellent stability and precision. They are commonly used in high-voltage applications and have a wider range of current carrying capacities.</p></p>
