<p><p>Expanding plug valves are available with two types of operating mechanisms: HandWheel Operated and Gear Operated. HandWheel Operated plug valves are manually operated by turning a handwheel, which controls the opening and closing of the valve. This type of valve is commonly used in applications where quick operation is required. On the other hand, Gear Operated plug valves are operated using gears and a gearbox, allowing for more precise control over the valve's position. These valves are often used in applications where precision and fine-tuning are necessary.</p></p>
