<p><p>VOC Detection Equipment Market encompasses various types of devices used to detect volatile organic compounds (VOCs) in the environment. Two common types include PID (Photoionization Detection) and Metal-oxide Semiconductor (MOS) detectors. PID detectors measure VOCs by ionizing them with ultraviolet light and detecting the resulting electric current. They offer high sensitivity and detection capabilities. MOS detectors utilize a semiconductor sensor to detect VOCs based on changes in electrical resistance. They are cost-effective and suitable for continuous monitoring applications. Both types play a crucial role in industrial safety, environmental regulations, and air quality monitoring.</p></p>
