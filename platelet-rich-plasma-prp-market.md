<p><strong>The Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P-PRP</li><li>L-PRP</li><li>L-PRF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) is a medical technique that uses a patient's own blood to stimulate tissue repair and regeneration. There are different types of PRP available in the market. P-PRP (Pure PRP) refers to the extraction of platelets from the blood using a centrifuge, which are then injected back into the body. L-PRP (Leukocyte-rich PRP) involves the inclusion of white blood cells along with platelets. On the other hand, L-PRF (Leukocyte and Platelet-rich Fibrin) involves the conversion of blood into a fibrin matrix, containing platelets and leukocytes, for a more sustained release of growth factors.</p></p>
