<p><p>The rare gas purifier market is classified into three types: titanium tube, copper oxide tube, and molecular sieve tube. Titanium tube purifiers utilize titanium as a reactive catalyst to remove impurities. Copper oxide tube purifiers rely on copper oxide as a reactive substance to eliminate contaminants. Molecular sieve tube purifiers work by using a specialized material with highly porous structures to selectively absorb unwanted particles. These different types cater to specific requirements and preferences of consumers in the rare gas purification industry.</p></p>
