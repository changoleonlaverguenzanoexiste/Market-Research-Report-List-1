<p><p>The Artificial Intelligence (AI) Chips market can be categorized into four types: GPU, ASIC, FPGA, and CPU. GPUs (Graphics Processing Units) are primarily designed for rendering images but are increasingly used in AI training due to their ability to handle parallel processing effectively. ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuits) are custom-built chips tailored for specific AI tasks, providing higher performance and energy efficiency. FPGAs (Field-Programmable Gate Arrays) are flexible chips that can be reconfigured, allowing optimization for different AI algorithms. CPUs (Central Processing Units) are general-purpose chips that are less specialized for AI but commonly used for AI inference tasks.</p></p>
