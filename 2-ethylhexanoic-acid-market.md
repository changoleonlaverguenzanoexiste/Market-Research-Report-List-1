<p><p>2-Ethylhexanoic acid is primarily produced through two methods: Butyraldehyde method and Octanol method. The Butyraldehyde method involves the oxidation of butyraldehyde with the use of catalysts to form 2-Ethylhexanoic acid. On the other hand, the Octanol method includes the hydroformylation of octene followed by oxidation to produce the acid.
