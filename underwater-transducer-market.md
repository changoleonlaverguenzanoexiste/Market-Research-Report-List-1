<p><strong>The Underwater Transducer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>Intermediate Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The underwater transducer market is categorized into three types: low frequency, intermediate frequency, and high frequency. Low frequency transducers operate at frequencies below 100 kHz, making them suitable for long-range underwater communication and sonar applications. Intermediate frequency transducers operate between 100 kHz and 500 kHz, offering a balance between range and resolution for imaging and detection purposes. High frequency transducers operate above 500 kHz, providing excellent resolution for underwater imaging and detailed object detection at close range. These different types cater to various underwater applications based on their specific frequency ranges.</p></p>
