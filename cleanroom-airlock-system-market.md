<p><p>Cleanroom airlock system is an essential component of controlled environments, such as research facilities, hospitals, or pharmaceutical factories, to minimize contamination risks. Cascading airlocks operate by creating a series of chambers with increasing air pressure, preventing contaminants from entering clean areas. Bubble airlocks use a continuous stream of air to maintain cleanliness during personnel entry and exit. Sink airlocks have a sink inside to allow for hand washing while maintaining hygiene. Other types may include pass-through airlocks and vestibule airlocks, which offer unique features and functions to suit specific cleanroom requirements.</p></p>
