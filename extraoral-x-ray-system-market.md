<p><p>The extraoral X-ray system market includes two types of systems: digital panoramic X-ray systems and panoramic/cephalometric X-ray systems. On the other hand, panoramic/cephalometric X-ray systems offer both panoramic and cephalometric imaging, allowing dentists to obtain a wider range of diagnostic information for orthodontic and maxillofacial treatments.
