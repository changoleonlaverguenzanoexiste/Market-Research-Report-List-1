<p><p>Recycling plants can be classified into two main types: automatic and semi-automatic markets. Automatic recycling plants are fully mechanized facilities that use advanced technologies and machinery to process recyclable materials efficiently. These plants require minimal human intervention and are designed to handle large volumes of waste materials. On the other hand, semi-automatic recycling plants employ a combination of manual labor and machinery. While some tasks are automated, others require human intervention for sorting, segregating, and processing recyclables. These plants are suitable for smaller operations or areas with limited resources.</p></p>
