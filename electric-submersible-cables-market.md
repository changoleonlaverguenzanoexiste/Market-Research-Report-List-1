<p><p>The electric submersible cables market comprises two prominent types: ESP flat power cable and ESP round power cable. ESP flat power cables are designed for use in downhole submersible oil and gas pumps, where they provide power transmission. On the other hand, ESP round power cables are also used in downhole submersible applications but offer features like enhanced flexibility and resistance to abrasion and chemicals. Both cable types play a crucial role in ensuring efficient power transmission for submersible oil and gas pumping systems.</p></p>
