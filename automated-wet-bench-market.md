<p><p>The automated wet bench market includes two main types: fully automated and semi-automated systems. A fully automated wet bench system operates without manual intervention, utilizing robotics and advanced technology to perform various wet processes. This type offers high efficiency, precision, and reliability, making it suitable for complex applications. On the other hand, a semi-automated wet bench system involves a combination of manual and automated processes.
