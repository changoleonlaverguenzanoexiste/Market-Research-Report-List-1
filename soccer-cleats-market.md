<p><p>The soccer cleats market can be categorized into different types based on the playing surface. FG soccer cleats, also known as firm ground cleats, are designed for natural grass pitches. SG soccer cleats, or soft ground cleats, have longer studs for wet and muddy grounds. HG soccer cleats, or hard ground cleats, are for dry, hard surfaces. TF soccer cleats, or turf soccer cleats, are meant for artificial turf. AG soccer cleats, or artificial grass cleats, are specifically designed for synthetic grass surfaces. Each type caters to different playing conditions and surfaces.</p></p>
