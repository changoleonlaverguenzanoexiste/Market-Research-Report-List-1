<p><strong>The Dental Milling Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Type</li><li>Vertical Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental milling machine market is classified into two types: horizontal type and vertical type. Horizontal type milling machines have a horizontal spindle orientation and are typically used for larger dental restorations, such as bridges or full-arch implant-supported dentures. Vertical type milling machines have a vertical spindle orientation and are commonly used for smaller dental restorations, such as individual crowns or veneers. The choice between these two types depends on the size and complexity of the dental restorations required by the dental professionals.</p></p>
