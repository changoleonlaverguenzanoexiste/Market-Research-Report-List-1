<p><p>In Litecoin trading, there are three main market types: Pay to Public Key Hash (P2PKH), Pay to Public Key (P2PK), and Pay to Script Hash (P2SH). P2PKH refers to transactions where funds are sent to the hashed public key of the recipient. P2PK involves sending funds directly to the public key of the recipient. P2SH involves transactions where funds are sent to a script hash, allowing more complex conditions for spending the funds. These market types determine how funds are securely transferred in Litecoin trading.</p></p>
