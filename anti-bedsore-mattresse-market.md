<p><ul><li>Static Type</li><li>Dynamic Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti bedsore mattresses are designed to prevent bedsores by reducing pressure on the body. They can be categorized into two types: static and dynamic. Static mattresses use layers of foam or air to distribute pressure evenly, providing a comfortable surface. Dynamic mattresses, on the other hand, have adjustable air chambers that continuously inflate and deflate, mimicking natural body movements and offering enhanced pressure relief. Both types aim to reduce the risk of bedsores, but dynamic mattresses provide the added benefit of customizable pressure adjustments for individual needs.</p></p>
