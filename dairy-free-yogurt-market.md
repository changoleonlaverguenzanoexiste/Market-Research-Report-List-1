<p><p>Dairy-free yogurt, as the name suggests, is a non-dairy alternative to traditional yogurt that is made without milk or any other dairy ingredients. The increasing prevalence of lactose intolerance and the growing adoption of vegan and dairy-free diets are the key factors driving the market growth. It is also fueled by rising consumer awareness about the health benefits of dairy-free products, including improved digestion and gut health.</p><p>Moreover, innovations in product formulations and flavors have played a crucial role in attracting a wider consumer base.
