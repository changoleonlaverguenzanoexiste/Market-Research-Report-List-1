<p><p>The Laboratory Automatic Liquid Handling System market offers two types of systems, namely Fully Automatic and Semi-automatic. Fully Automatic systems are designed to handle liquids without manual intervention, performing tasks such as sample preparation and dispensing with high precision and efficiency. On the other hand, Semi-automatic systems require some level of human intervention, where operators manually load samples or adjust settings. Both types cater to the needs of laboratories, but Fully Automatic systems offer a higher level of automation and eliminate human error to a greater extent.</p></p>
