<p><p>The Ultrasound Probe Disinfection System Market is categorized into two types: High-level Disinfection and Intermediate/Low-level Disinfection. High-level disinfection refers to the process of killing or eliminating all types of microorganisms, including bacteria, viruses, and fungi, reducing the risk of infection transmission. Intermediate/Low-level disinfection, on the other hand, involves eliminating most bacteria and some viruses, but may not be effective against certain types of resistant microorganisms. Both types of disinfection systems are used in the market to ensure the safety and cleanliness of ultrasound probes.</p></p>
