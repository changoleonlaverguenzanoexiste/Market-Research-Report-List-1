<p><p>The HVAC Temperature Sensor Market includes different types of sensors used to measure temperature in heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. NTC sensors (Negative Temperature Coefficient) have electrical resistance that decreases as temperature increases. PTC sensors (Positive Temperature Coefficient) experience resistance growth with temperature rise. RTD sensors (Resistance Temperature Detectors) use the principle of resistance change with temperature. Thermocouples (TCs) generate voltage proportional to temperature difference between two junctions. Other temperature sensors may include infrared sensors or MEMS-based sensors. These various types cater to different applications and preferences in the HVAC industry.</p></p>
