<p><p>Ambient energy harvesters are devices that capture and convert energy from the surrounding environment into usable electrical power. There is also a growing emphasis on improving the efficiency and scalability of energy harvesting systems, enabling their integration into a wide range of applications.</p><p>Overall, the ambient energy harvester market is poised for substantial growth in the coming years.
