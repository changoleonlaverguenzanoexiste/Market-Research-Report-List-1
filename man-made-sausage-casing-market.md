<p><p>The man-made sausage casing market is segmented into four types: Collagen Casing, Cellulose Casing, Plastic Casing, and Others. Collagen casing is made from animal collagen and is widely used due to its high strength and flexibility. Cellulose casing is popular for its edible properties and resistance to heat. Plastic casing, made from materials like polyethylene, is known for its durability. The Others category includes casings made from materials such as fibrous and alginate, offering unique properties to suit different sausage production needs.</p></p>
