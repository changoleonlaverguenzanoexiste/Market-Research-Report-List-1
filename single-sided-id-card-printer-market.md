<p><p>The Single-Sided ID Card Printer market is divided into two types: Inkjet Printers and Dye Sub Printers. </p><p>Inkjet Printers are known for their versatility and are popular for printing high-quality graphics and photos on ID cards. They use ink cartridges to spray droplets of ink onto the card's surface, resulting in vibrant and smooth prints.</p><p>On the other hand, Dye Sub Printers use a different technique. They transfer a dye layer onto the card using a heat process, resulting in professional-looking, durable, and long-lasting prints. They are often preferred in industries where high-quality images and durability are crucial, such as in government agencies or corporations.</p></p>
