<p><strong>The Tractor Transmission System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Hydraulic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tractor transmission system market primarily consists of two types: mechanical and hydraulic. Mechanical transmission systems utilize gears and shafts to transfer power from the engine to the wheels. They offer good power transfer efficiency but can be challenging to operate and require manual shifting. On the other hand, hydraulic transmission systems use hydraulic fluids to transfer power and offer smoother operation and automatic shifting. However, they tend to have lower power transfer efficiency compared to mechanical systems. Both systems play a key role in providing the necessary power and control for tractors.</p></p>
