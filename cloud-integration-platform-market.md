<p><p>Cloud integration platform market types can be classified into three categories: public cloud, private cloud, and hybrid cloud. In the public cloud market, the platform is owned and operated by a third-party provider and accessible by multiple organizations or individuals over the internet. Conversely, the private cloud market involves platforms that are exclusively owned and operated by a single organization, ensuring greater control and security. Hybrid cloud market combines both public and private clouds, allowing organizations to leverage the benefits of both while maintaining flexibility and control over their data and applications.</p></p>
