<p><p>The Lithium-ion (Li-ion) Battery Materials market consists of two main types - Cathode Materials and Anode Materials. Cathode Materials are responsible for the positive electrode and play a crucial role in storing and releasing lithium ions during the battery's charging and discharging cycles. Anode Materials, on the other hand, occupy the negative electrode and are responsible for receiving and releasing electrons during the battery's operation. Both Cathode and Anode Materials are essential components in Li-ion batteries, contributing to their overall performance and efficiency.</p></p>
