<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Smart Gas Meter Market consists of two main types: Automatic Meter Reading (AMR) and Advanced Metering Infrastructure (AMI). AMR technology enables gas consumption data to be collected remotely using mobile or fixed networks. It simplifies meter reading, improves billing accuracy, and facilitates timely problem detection. AMI, on the other hand, utilizes two-way communication systems to transmit gas usage data in real-time. This enables utilities to monitor and manage gas distribution more efficiently, while providing consumers with detailed information for better energy management.</p></p>
