<p><p>RegTech software market can be classified into two types: on-premises and cloud-based. On-premises refers to software that is installed and operated on the company's own servers, providing more control over data and security, but requiring higher upfront costs and maintenance. On the other hand, cloud-based RegTech software is hosted and accessed through the internet, offering flexibility, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as it eliminates the need for hardware and reduces maintenance requirements.
