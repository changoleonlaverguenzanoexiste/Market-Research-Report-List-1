<p><p>Electric Resistance Welded (ERW) pipes are a type of steel pipes that are made from strip or coil being rolled into a tube shape and then welded longitudinally. The welding process used is a high-frequency electric current which is passed through the edges of the strip or coil, causing them to heat and join together. ERW pipes are commonly used in various industries such as oil and gas, petrochemicals, power generation, and automotive, due to their high strength, durability, and ability to withstand high pressure and temperature.</p><p>The Electric Resistance Welded (ERW) Pipes Market is expected to witness steady growth during the forecast period.
