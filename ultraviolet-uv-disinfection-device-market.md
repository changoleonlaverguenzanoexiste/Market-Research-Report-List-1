<p><p>The UV Disinfection Device market is segmented into three types: Low Pressure Lamp System, Low Pressure High Strength Lamp System, and Medium Pressure Lamp System. </p><p>The Low Pressure Lamp System type utilizes low-pressure mercury lamps to emit UV-C light for disinfection purposes. The Low Pressure High Strength Lamp System type employs high-strength low-pressure mercury lamps, which produce higher levels of UV-C light for more intensive disinfection. The Medium Pressure Lamp System type utilizes medium-pressure mercury lamps, which emit a broad range of UV wavelengths for disinfection and are particularly effective against a wider range of microorganisms.</p></p>
