<p><p>Nuclear Moisture Separator Reheaters (MSRs) are used in nuclear power plants to remove moisture from steam before it enters the turbine. There are two main types of MSRs: Horizontal MSR and Vertical MSR. </p><p>Horizontal MSRs are designed to separate moisture from steam in a horizontal direction, while Vertical MSRs perform the same function in a vertical orientation. Both types of MSR play a crucial role in maintaining the efficiency and performance of nuclear power plants by ensuring that only dry steam enters the turbine.</p></p>
