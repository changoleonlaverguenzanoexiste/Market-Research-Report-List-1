<p><p>Commercial RAC PD Compressors are used in the air conditioning industry. Reciprocating Type compressors use a piston and cylinder mechanism to compress refrigerant gas. They are known for their high efficiency and durability, making them suitable for large-scale cooling systems. Scroll Type compressors, on the other hand, use two interlocking scrolls to compress refrigerant gas. They are more compact and have fewer moving parts, resulting in quieter operation and higher reliability. Both types serve the same purpose of compressing refrigerant gas but differ in design and performance characteristics.</p></p>
