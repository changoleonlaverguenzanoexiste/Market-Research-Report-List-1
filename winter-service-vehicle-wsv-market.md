Blowers are designed to blow away snow using forced air. Displacement plows are equipped with plows that can be hydraulically adjusted to push snow to the side of the road. Rotary brooms have rotating brushes that sweep away snow. Other types of WSVs include salt spreaders, sanders, and combination units that combine multiple functions for efficient winter maintenance. These vehicles are essential for maintaining safe and navigable roads during winter conditions.</p></p>
