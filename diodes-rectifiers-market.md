<p><p>The diodes and rectifiers market consists of various types of diodes used in different applications. Bridge rectifiers are designed to convert alternating current (AC) to direct current (DC) and are widely used in power supplies. PIN diodes are used in high-frequency applications such as amplifiers and switches. TVS diodes/ESD suppressors protect electronic equipment from voltage spikes and electrostatic discharge. Current regulator diodes are used to maintain a constant current in a circuit. The "Others" segment includes other specialized diodes used in specific applications such as laser diodes, photodiodes, and schottky diodes.</p></p>
