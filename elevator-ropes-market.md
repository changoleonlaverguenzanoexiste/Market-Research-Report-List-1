<p><p>Elevator ropes are essential components in the vertical transportation industry. There are different types of elevator ropes available in the market. One type is Fiber Core (FC), which consists of a central core made of synthetic fibers surrounded by steel wires. Another type is Independent Wire Rope Core (IWRC), where the core is made of a separate wire rope strand. Additionally, there are other types of elevator ropes available in the market, which are not specifically categorized as FC or IWRC. These different types of elevator ropes offer varying features and benefits, providing options for different elevator systems.</p></p>
