<p><strong>The Self-Levelling Concrete Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Underlayments</li><li>Toppings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-levelling concrete is commonly used in two main markets: underlayments and toppings. Underlayments are used as a smooth and level surface for floor coverings, such as tiles or carpets, to be installed upon. They help to correct uneven or sloping substrates. Toppings are applied as a finishing layer on existing concrete surfaces to enhance durability, aesthetics, or functionality. Both types of self-levelling concrete offer quick and efficient solutions for achieving flat and even surfaces in construction and renovation projects.</p></p>
