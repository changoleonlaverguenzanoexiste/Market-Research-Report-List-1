<p><p>Passive earmuffs for shooting are traditional, non-electronic devices that provide protection by blocking out loud noises. They use padding and insulation to reduce the intensity of sounds. On the other hand, active earmuffs use electronic technology to actively suppress harmful noises while still allowing communication and low-level sounds to be heard. These earmuffs typically have built-in microphones that amplify softer sounds and speakers that emit noise-canceling frequencies to protect the shooter's ears. Both types cater to the shooting market, but active earmuffs offer more advanced features for enhanced safety and convenience.</p></p>
