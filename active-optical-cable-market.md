<p><p>Active Optical Cable (AOC) is a type of cable that uses optical fibers to transmit data signals. Unlike traditional copper cables, AOCs incorporate optical transceivers at both ends, enabling high-speed data transfer over longer distances. These cables are lightweight, flexible, and offer excellent signal quality, making them suitable for applications such as data centers, consumer electronics, and telecommunications.</p><p>The future of the Active Optical Cable market looks promising. With the increasing demand for high-speed and reliable data transmission, AOCs are expected to witness significant adoption in various industries. The market forecast suggests that the demand for AOCs will continue to rise as organizations seek faster data transmission solutions to meet the evolving technological requirements.
