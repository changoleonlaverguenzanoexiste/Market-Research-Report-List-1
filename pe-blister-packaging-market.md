<p><p>PE blister packaging is a type of packaging made from Polyethylene (PE) material. There are two main market types in the PE blister packaging industry. Thermoforming is a process where plastic sheets are heated until pliable, then molded into a desired shape using a vacuum or pressure method. This type of packaging is commonly used for products with complex shapes. Cold forming, on the other hand, involves the use of aluminum-based material for the blister, forming it at room temperature. This type of packaging is commonly used for products that require higher barrier properties.</p></p>
