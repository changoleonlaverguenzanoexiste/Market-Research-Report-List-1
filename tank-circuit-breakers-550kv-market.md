<p><p>Tank circuit breakers are high voltage circuit breakers used in electrical power systems. They are classified into two types: dead tank circuit breakers and live tank circuit breakers. Dead tank circuit breakers have the interrupting unit enclosed in a grounded metal tank, while live tank circuit breakers have the interrupting unit exposed to the air. These breakers are designed to interrupt fault currents and protect electrical systems from damage. The market for tank circuit breakers with a voltage rating of ≥550KV includes both dead tank and live tank circuit breakers.</p></p>
