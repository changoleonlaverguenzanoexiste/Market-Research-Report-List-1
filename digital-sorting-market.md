<p><p>The digital sorting market consists of various types of sorting technologies used in industries. Cameras are commonly used for image-based sorting, capturing images of objects and classifying them based on predetermined criteria. Lasers are utilized for size, shape, and color sorting, emitting beams to measure and differentiate objects. NIR sorters employ near-infrared light to determine the composition and quality of materials. Hyperspectral cameras use advanced imaging techniques to analyze objects based on their spectral properties. Combined sorters integrate multiple technologies to achieve higher accuracy and efficiency in sorting operations.</p></p>
