<p><p>In the stainless steel solenoid valve market, there are two main types: Normally Open and Normally Closed. Normally Open solenoid valves remain open when they are not energized, allowing the flow of fluid through the valve. When energized, the solenoid closes the valve, stopping the flow. On the other hand, Normally Closed solenoid valves remain closed when not energized, cutting off the fluid flow. When energized, the valve opens, allowing fluid to pass through. These types serve different purposes in various industries depending on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
