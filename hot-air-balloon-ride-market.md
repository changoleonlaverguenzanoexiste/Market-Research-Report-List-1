<p><p>The hot air balloon ride market can be divided into two types: tethered flight and free flight. This type is popular for events or festivals where passengers can enjoy views from a fixed location. Free flight, on the other hand, offers a more adventurous experience as the balloon is untethered, allowing it to float freely in the sky. Passengers can enjoy a longer and more immersive journey, typically lasting one to two hours.</p></p>
