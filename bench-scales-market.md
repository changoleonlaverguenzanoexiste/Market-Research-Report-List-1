<p><ul><li>Digital Type</li><li>Analog Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bench scales are used for weighing objects on a flat surface. There are two main types of bench scales: digital and analog. Digital bench scales have a built-in digital display which shows the weight measurement in numerical form. They are known for their accuracy and precision, making them ideal for industries that require exact measurements. On the other hand, analog bench scales use a needle or pointer on a dial to indicate the weight. They are less precise but can still provide an approximate measurement, making them suitable for less demanding applications.</p></p>
