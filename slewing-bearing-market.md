<p><p>The slewing bearing market consists of three types: external gear type, internal gear type, and non-gear type. External gear type slewing bearings have gears attached to their outer diameter to enable rotation. Internal gear type slewing bearings have gears on their inner diameter, allowing for smooth movement. Non-gear type slewing bearings, on the other hand, do not have any gears and rely on external parts for rotation. These different types cater to diverse industrial applications, providing various options for users based on their specific needs.</p></p>
