<p><p>The flavor enhancers market encompasses various types of ingredients that enhance the taste of food products. Glutamates, such as monosodium glutamate (MSG), enhance umami flavors. Yeast extracts, made from yeast cells, are rich in nutrients and contribute to the overall flavor profile of foods. These flavor enhancers are used in a wide range of food products to improve taste and appeal to consumers.</p></p>
