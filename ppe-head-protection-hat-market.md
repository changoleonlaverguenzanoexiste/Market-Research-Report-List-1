<p><p>The PPE head protection hat market consists of two main types: hard hats and bump caps. Hard hats are designed to provide sturdy protection against falling objects, electrical shocks, and penetration injuries in construction and industrial settings. They have a rigid outer shell and cushioning material inside. Bump caps, on the other hand, offer limited protection and are more suitable for low-risk environments. They are lightweight and have a softer shell to prevent bumps and bruises caused by minor impacts. Both types fulfill specific safety requirements in different work environments.</p></p>
