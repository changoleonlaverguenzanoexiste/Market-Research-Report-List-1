<p><strong>The Plastic Glove Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inert Gas Glove Box</li><li>Isolation Glove Box</li><li>Anaerobic Glove Box</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic glove box market consists of three main types: inert gas glove box, isolation glove box, and anaerobic glove box. An inert gas glove box is used to manipulate substances sensitive to oxygen, moisture, or other atmospheric conditions. Isolation glove boxes are designed to provide a sterile environment and prevent contamination. Anaerobic glove boxes create a low-oxygen or oxygen-free atmosphere for handling materials that react to oxygen. These types of glove boxes cater to diverse needs, providing controlled environments for various scientific and industrial applications.</p></p>
