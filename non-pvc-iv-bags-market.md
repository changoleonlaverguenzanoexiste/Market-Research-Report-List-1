<p><p>Non-PVC IV bags are available in two types: single-chambered and multi-chambered. Single-chambered IV bags contain only one compartment for holding fluid, making them suitable for administering a single medication or solution. On the other hand, multi-chambered IV bags have multiple compartments, allowing for the simultaneous administration of different medications or solutions. Both types of IV bags are preferred by healthcare facilities that prioritize patient safety and environmental sustainability by avoiding the use of PVC materials.</p></p>
