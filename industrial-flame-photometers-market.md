<p><ul><li>Single Channel</li><li>Dual Channel</li><li>Multi Channel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial flame photometers are analytical instruments used to measure the concentration of various elements in a sample through flame emission spectroscopy. Single channel flame photometers have a single flame and detector, allowing measurement of one element at a time. Dual channel photometers have two separate flames and detectors, enabling simultaneous measurement of two elements. Multichannel photometers consist of multiple flames and detectors, enabling measurement of several elements simultaneously. These different types of flame photometers cater to varying analytical needs and offer flexibility in elemental analysis for industrial applications.</p></p>
