<p><p>Semi-Automatic Stretch Wrappers are used for packaging products securely. There are three main types in the market. The Rotary Stretch Wrapper is suitable for irregularly shaped or heavy items and wraps them continuously in a rotating motion. The Turntable Stretch Wrapper works by placing the load on a turntable and spinning it while the film is dispensed to wrap the product. Lastly, the Robotic Stretch Wrapper uses robotic arms to wrap the load, providing flexibility and precision in the wrapping process. Each type caters to different packaging needs.</p></p>
