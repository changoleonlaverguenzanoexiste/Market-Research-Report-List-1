<p><p>The Motor Intelligent Module market consists of two main types: Half-Bridge Driver and Full-Bridge Driver. The Half-Bridge Driver module is designed to control the current flow in half of a motor bridge circuit, allowing for bidirectional control. On the other hand, the Full-Bridge Driver module controls the current flow in the entire motor bridge circuit, providing more precise control over motor movement. Both types of modules are crucial in various applications, such as robotics, electric vehicles, and industrial automation, enabling efficient and reliable motor control.</p></p>
