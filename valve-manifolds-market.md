<p><strong>The Valve Manifolds Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Two Way Mainfold Valves</li><li>Three Way Mainfold Valves</li><li>Five Way Mainfold Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Valve manifolds are categorized into different types based on the number of ways they can control the flow of liquids or gases. Two-way manifold valves are designed to allow or block the flow in a single direction. Three-way manifold valves provide options for flow in two directions, typically used for diverter or selector applications. Five-way manifold valves offer even more versatility by enabling flow in multiple directions, commonly used for distribution or mixing purposes in industrial settings.</p></p>
