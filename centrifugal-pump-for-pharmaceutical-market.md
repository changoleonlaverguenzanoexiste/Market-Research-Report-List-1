Single suction pumps have one inlet for fluid, while double suction pumps have two inlets. Single suction pumps are cost-effective and suitable for low to medium flow rates. Double suction pumps offer higher efficiency and are ideal for large flow applications. Both types are widely utilized in the pharmaceutical market for various fluid transfer needs.</p></p>
