<p><p>Automotive Non-Contact Temperature Sensors come in various types. Thermistors are temperature-sensitive resistors. Resistance Temperature Detectors use the resistance of metal to measure temperature. Thermocouples generate a voltage proportional to temperature difference between two points. IC Temperature Sensors utilize integrated circuit technology. MEMS Temperature Sensors use microscopic mechanical structures to sense temperature. Infrared Sensors detect temperature by measuring infrared radiation. Each type has its own unique characteristics and applications in the automotive industry for non-contact temperature measurement.</p></p>
