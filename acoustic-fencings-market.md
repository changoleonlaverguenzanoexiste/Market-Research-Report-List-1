<p><p>Acoustic fencings, also known as sound barriers or noise barriers, are designed to reduce or block the transmission of sound waves. Furthermore, stringent regulations by government authorities to limit noise levels have further propelled the market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the acoustic fencings market is the adoption of advanced technologies and innovative materials.
