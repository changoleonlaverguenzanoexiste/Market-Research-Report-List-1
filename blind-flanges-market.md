<p><ul><li>FF</li><li>RF</li><li>MFM</li><li>TG</li><li>RJ</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blind flanges are important components used in piping systems to seal the end of a pipe. They come in various market types, including FF (Flat Face), RF (Raised Face), MFM (Male and Female), TG (Tongue and Groove), and RJ (Ring Joint). FF flanges have a flat sealing surface, while RF flanges have a raised sealing surface. MFM flanges use a combination of male and female flanges for a tight seal. TG flanges have a tongue and groove design for enhanced sealing. RJ flanges use a metal ring to create a high-pressure seal.</p></p>
