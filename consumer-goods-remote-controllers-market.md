<p><p>Consumer goods remote controllers refer to the devices used to operate electronic devices from a distance. Standard remote controllers are the traditional ones that require manual input using buttons or knobs. Motion-controlled remote controllers use sensors to detect movements and gestures, allowing for a more interactive experience. Voice-controlled remote controllers utilize voice recognition technology, enabling users to control their devices through verbal commands.
