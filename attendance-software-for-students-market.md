<p><p>Attendance software for students is available in two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based software operates on the internet and allows students to access their attendance records and submit attendance remotely. It is cost-effective and offers easy scalability. On-premises software, on the other hand, is installed locally on the school's servers, requiring maintenance and updates. It provides schools with full control over their data and may be preferred by institutions with strict data security policies. Both market types offer different advantages, allowing schools to choose the solution that best fits their needs.</p></p>
