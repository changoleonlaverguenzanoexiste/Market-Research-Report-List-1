<p><p>The mosquito lamp market comprises three main types: electronic mosquito killer lamps, air suction mosquito killer lamps, and adhesive mosquito killer lamps. Electronic mosquito killer lamps use ultraviolet light to attract mosquitoes and an electric grid to kill them upon contact. Air suction mosquito killer lamps use a fan to trap mosquitoes in a container, where they are eventually killed. Adhesive mosquito killer lamps use sticky pads to trap and ultimately eliminate mosquitoes. Each type offers its unique features and effectiveness in combating mosquito infestation.</p></p>
