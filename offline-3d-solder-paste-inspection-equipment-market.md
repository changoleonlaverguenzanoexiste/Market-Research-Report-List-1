<p><p>The offline 3D solder paste inspection equipment market consists of two main types: laser-based and PMP-based equipment. Laser-based equipment utilizes laser technology to measure and inspect solder paste deposits. It offers high accuracy in detecting defects and ensuring quality soldering. On the other hand, PMP-based equipment uses structured light patterns to examine the solder paste on circuit boards. It provides quick and precise inspection results, allowing manufacturers to identify and address any issues with solder paste application efficiently. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the reliability and performance of electronic devices.</p></p>
