<p><p>Hearable electronic devices, also known as hearables, refer to small wearable devices that are designed to be worn in or around the ears. This trend is likely to enhance user experience and increase the adoption of hearables in various consumer segments.</p><p>Another trend observed is the growing interest in health and fitness monitoring features. Hearables with built-in sensors can track various health parameters such as heart rate, sleep patterns, and exercise performance.
