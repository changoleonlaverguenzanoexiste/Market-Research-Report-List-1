<p><p>Electrophotographic printing is a widely used printing technology categorized into various types. Offset printing is the most common method for high-volume production, using metal plates and rubber blankets. Gravure printing involves engraved cylinders and is ideal for long runs or packaging materials. Flexography uses flexible plates and is suitable for printing on various surfaces like plastics and cardboard. Screen printing is ideal for printing on textiles and materials with uneven surfaces. Letterpress printing uses raised surfaces for direct ink application and is often used for wedding invitations or business cards.</p></p>
