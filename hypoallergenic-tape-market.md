<p><p>Hypoallergenic tape refers to a type of tape that is less likely to cause allergic reactions on the skin. There are two main market types in this category: paper tape and adhesive tape. Adhesive tape, on the other hand, is made of a strong adhesive that sticks well to the skin, making it suitable for securing dressings or medical devices.
