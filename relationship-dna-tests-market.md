<p><p>Relationship DNA tests are genetic tests used to determine the biological connection between individuals. Siblings DNA tests analyze the DNA markers shared between potential siblings to determine if they have the same biological parents. Grandparentage tests examine the DNA of potential grandparents and grandchildren to establish their biological relationship. Genetic reconstruction tests utilize the DNA of family members to reconstruct the genetic makeup of an individual, thus determining biological connections. Other markets in this field may include tests for avuncular relationships (aunt/uncle relationship) or tests for determining maternity or paternity.</p></p>
