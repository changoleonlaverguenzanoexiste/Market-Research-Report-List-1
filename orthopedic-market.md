Additionally, the rising geriatric population, along with a growing number of sports-related injuries, has driven the demand for orthopedic procedures and devices. Moreover, the increasing prevalence of chronic conditions such as arthritis and osteoporosis further fuels market growth.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the orthopedic market is the adoption of robot-assisted surgeries.
