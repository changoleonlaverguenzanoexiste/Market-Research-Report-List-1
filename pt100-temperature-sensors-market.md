<p><p>The Pt100 Temperature Sensors market consists of three types: 2-wire Pt100 Temperature Sensors, 3-wire Pt100 Temperature Sensors, and 4-wire Pt100 Temperature Sensors. </p><p>2-wire sensors are commonly used due to their simplicity and cost-effectiveness, but they may experience measurement inaccuracies due to wire resistance. </p><p>3-wire sensors aim to minimize this issue by using an additional wire for compensating wire resistance. </p><p>4-wire sensors offer the highest accuracy as they have separate wires for current and voltage, eliminating any measurement error caused by wire resistance.</p></p>
