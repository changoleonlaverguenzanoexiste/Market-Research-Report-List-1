<p><p>The plant protein ingredient market can be divided into two types: organic and conventional. They are produced in accordance with organic farming practices, which prioritize sustainability and environmental protection. On the other hand, conventional plant protein ingredients can come from plants that may have been exposed to synthetic chemicals, pesticides, or GMOs during cultivation. These ingredients are produced using more traditional farming methods but may not meet organic certification standards.</p></p>
