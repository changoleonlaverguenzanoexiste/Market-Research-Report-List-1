<p><strong>The Background Music System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central</li><li>Split</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Background Music System market can be classified into two types: Central and Split. In a Central background music system, a single source or player is used to distribute music to multiple zones or areas. This allows for centralized control and easy management of the music. On the other hand, in a Split background music system, different sources or players are used for each zone or area. This enables customized music selection for each specific area, providing more flexibility and personalization.</p></p>
