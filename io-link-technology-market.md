<p><strong>The IO-Link Technology Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IO-Link Masters</li><li>IO-Link Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IO-Link technology market consists of two main types: IO-Link Masters and IO-Link Devices. IO-Link Masters are the central devices that enable communication between the field devices and the higher-level control system. They act as a bridge, collecting data from IO-Link Devices and transmitting it to the control system. IO-Link Devices, on the other hand, are the field devices such as sensors, actuators, and switches that connect to the IO-Link Master. They provide feedback, collect data, and enable control functions at the field level, enhancing flexibility, and improving efficiency in industrial automation systems.</p></p>
