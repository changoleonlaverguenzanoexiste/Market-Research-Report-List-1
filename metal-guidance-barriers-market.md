<p><p>Metal guidance barriers are used to create boundaries or direct traffic flow in various settings. There are three main types of metal guidance barriers: tape, rope, and chain. Tape barriers consist of metal posts with retractable tape that can be extended or retracted to control access. Rope barriers utilize metal poles with ropes running between them for a more elegant and decorative look. Chain barriers use metal chains connected to posts, providing a sturdy and durable solution for crowd control. These different types allow for versatility in choosing the most suitable barrier system for specific environments.</p></p>
