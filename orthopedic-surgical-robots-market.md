<p><p>Orthopedic surgical robots refer to robotic systems that assist surgeons in performing orthopedic surgeries with enhanced precision and efficiency. Among the key factors driving this growth is the rising prevalence of orthopedic disorders such as osteoarthritis and the aging population, which is more susceptible to such conditions. Additionally, the demand for minimally invasive surgeries, which reduce patient trauma, scarring, and recovery time, has also fueled the adoption of orthopedic surgical robots.</p><p>Furthermore, advancements in robotic technologies, such as the integration of artificial intelligence and machine learning, have improved the capabilities of orthopedic surgical robots. These technologies enable real-time monitoring, precise preoperative planning, and personalized patient-specific treatments, further boosting their demand.</p><p>The market is also witnessing a trend towards the development of collaborative robots or cobots.
