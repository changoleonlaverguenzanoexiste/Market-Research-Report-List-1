<p><p>Electrostatic Discharge Protection Diodes are designed to protect sensitive electronic components from damage caused by ESD events. Unidirectional ESD Protection Diodes allow current flow in one direction, providing protection from ESD threats in that direction. Bidirectional ESD Protection Diodes, on the other hand, allow current flow in both directions, providing protection from ESD threats in either direction. Both types of diodes are essential in preventing issues caused by electrostatic discharge in electronic devices and circuits.</p></p>
