<p><p>Vacuum tray dryers are widely used in various industries for drying purposes. They can be classified into different types based on the heating medium used: thermic fluid, steam, hot air, and electricity. Thermic fluid vacuum tray dryers utilize a heat transfer oil as the heating medium, which is circulated through a coil to heat the trays. Steam vacuum tray dryers use steam to directly heat the trays. Hot air vacuum tray dryers use hot air produced by a burner to dry the trays. Electricity-based vacuum tray dryers use electrical heating elements to generate heat for drying.</p></p>
