<p><p>Power transmission equipment market is classified into three types: electric power transmission equipment, mechanical power transmission equipment, and others. </p><p>Electric power transmission equipment refers to the various devices and components used for transmitting electrical power from power plants to distribution networks and end-users. This includes transformers, circuit breakers, and transmission lines. </p><p>Mechanical power transmission equipment involves machinery and devices that transfer mechanical power from a power source to various industrial applications. Examples include gears, belts, and couplings. </p><p>The category of "others" encompasses power transmission equipment that does not fall into the above two types, such as hydraulic and pneumatic power transmission systems.</p></p>
