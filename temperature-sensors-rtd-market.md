<p><p>Temperature sensors RTD (Resistance Temperature Detectors) are widely used in various industries for measuring temperature accurately. The market offers different types of RTDs including 2-wire, 3-wire, 4-wire, and others. </p><p>2-wire RTDs have two wires for both the excitation current and the resistance measurement, making them more susceptible to wire resistance errors. </p><p>3-wire RTDs introduce an additional wire to cancel out the resistance of the measurement lead wires, offering increased accuracy compared to 2-wire RTDs.</p><p>4-wire RTDs utilize four wires to separately measure the resistance and eliminate the lead wire resistance errors, providing the highest level of accuracy.</p><p>Other types of RTDs may include wireless or advanced digital versions that offer additional features and capabilities.</p></p>
