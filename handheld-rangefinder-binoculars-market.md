<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Handheld rangefinder binoculars are available in two market types: roof prism rangefinder binoculars and porro prism rangefinder binoculars. Roof prism binoculars have a straight barrel design, allowing light to pass through in a straight line, resulting in a sleeker and more compact structure. Porro prism binoculars, on the other hand, have an offset barrel design, which provides better depth perception and wider field of view. Both types offer rangefinder functionality, allowing users to accurately measure distances while also providing the benefits of binoculars for enhanced visibility.</p></p>
