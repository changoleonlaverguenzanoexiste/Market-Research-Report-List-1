<p><p>The 4K Set-top Box (STB) market comprises four main types: Satellite STBs, Hybrid STBs, Cable STBs, and IP STBs. Satellite STBs receive television signals through satellite transmission, offering a wide range of channels. Hybrid STBs combine satellite or cable with internet services, providing more options for users. Cable STBs receive television signals through cable networks, allowing users to access cable TV channels. IP STBs utilize internet protocol to stream content, enabling access to a variety of online services and video-on-demand platforms. Each type of STB caters to different user preferences and needs.</p></p>
