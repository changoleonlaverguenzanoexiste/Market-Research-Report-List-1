<p><p>Horizontal Boom Type boom sprayers are commonly used in agriculture for crop spraying. They feature a straight horizontal boom that extends out from the vehicle carrying the sprayer nozzles. Derrick Boom Type boom sprayers have a vertical mast with a boom attached at the top, allowing for more precise height control. Air Bag Type boom sprayers utilize air pressure to control the height of the boom, providing versatility in various terrains. Each of these boom sprayer types offers unique benefits and is designed for specific applications in the agricultural industry.</p></p>
