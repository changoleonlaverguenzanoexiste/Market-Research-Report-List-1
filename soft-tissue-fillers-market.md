<p><p>Soft tissue fillers are injectable substances that are used to restore volume and smooth out facial wrinkles and folds. There are different types of soft tissue fillers available in the market. Hyaluronic acid (HA) fillers are the most common and provide natural-looking results by retaining water in the skin. Calcium hydroxylapatite (CaHA) fillers stimulate collagen production and provide long-lasting effects. Poly-L-lactic acid (PLLA) fillers promote collagen growth gradually over time. Polymethylmethacrylate (PMMA) fillers are synthetic and provide permanent results. Other fillers include collagen-based and autologous fat injections. These various types cater to different patient needs and preferences.</p></p>
