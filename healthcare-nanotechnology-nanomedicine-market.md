<p><p>Healthcare Nanotechnology, also known as Nanomedicine, is the application of nanotechnology in the field of healthcare. Nanoparticles can be engineered to transport drugs directly to the diseased tissues or cells, minimizing the side effects and improving the efficacy of treatments.
