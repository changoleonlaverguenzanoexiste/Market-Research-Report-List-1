<p><p>The market for polyurethane spray machines can be categorized into three types: air-powered, electric, and hydraulic. Air-powered machines use compressed air to atomize and propel the polyurethane material onto the desired surface. Electric machines rely on electricity to power the spray system, making them more suitable for indoor or enclosed environments. Hydraulic machines use hydraulic fluid pressure to generate the necessary force for spraying polyurethane materials. These different machine types offer varied benefits and are used in various applications within the polyurethane industry.</p></p>
