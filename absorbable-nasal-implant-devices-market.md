<p><p>The absorbable nasal implant devices market is categorized into three types: polymer, carboxy methyl cellulose (CMC), and others. Polymer implants are made from biodegradable materials that gradually dissolve inside the body. Carboxy methyl cellulose (CMC) implants are composed of a naturally occurring polysaccharide that provides a temporary filler effect in the nasal area. The "others" category includes various absorbable materials used for nasal implant devices. These different types offer patients a range of options for addressing nasal deformities or irregularities, providing temporary support or structural modification as needed.</p></p>
