<p><p>The brake friction products market encompasses various types of components used in braking systems. Brake pads are widely employed, creating friction against the rotor to slow down or stop the vehicle. Brake shoes perform the same function but are used in drum brakes. Brake liners are often installed in heavy-duty applications such as commercial vehicles. Drum brakes use brake drums that rotate with the wheels, while disc brakes utilize brake discs that are clamped by the brake pads to stop the vehicle. These different products cater to diverse braking system requirements across various vehicles.</p></p>
