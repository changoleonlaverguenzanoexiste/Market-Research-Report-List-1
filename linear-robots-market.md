<p><p>Linear robots are categorized into three types: XY-X series, 2X-Y-Z series, and 2X-2Y-Z series. The XY-X series market focuses on robots that move on the XY plane and also have a third degree of freedom in the horizontal direction. The 2X-Y-Z series market involves robots capable of movement in the X, Y, and Z planes, providing more complex motion capabilities. Lastly, the 2X-2Y-Z series market includes robots that have two degrees of freedom in both the X and Y planes, as well as the Z-plane for complete multi-directional movement capabilities.</p></p>
