<p><ul><li>Manual Heat Press</li><li>Semi-automatic Heat Press</li><li>Automatic Heat Press</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat press market can be divided into three types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic heat presses. </p><p>Manual heat presses require users to manually operate the machine by adjusting pressure, time, and temperature settings. They are more suitable for small-scale operations or beginners due to their simplicity and lower cost.</p><p>Semi-automatic heat presses have some automation features, such as automatically opening and closing the press, reducing user effort. They are popular for medium-scale operations that require higher efficiency and consistency. </p><p>Automatic heat presses are fully automated, offering advanced features like programmable settings, digital displays, and auto-release mechanisms. These are ideal for large-scale operations, providing maximum productivity and precision.</p></p>
