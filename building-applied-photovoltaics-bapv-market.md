<p><p>Building Applied Photovoltaics (BAPV) refers to the integration of photovoltaic panels into the structures of buildings. This approach offers two major applications: rooftop and facades. In the rooftop application, solar panels are installed on the roof of buildings to convert sunlight into electricity. Facades, on the other hand, involve incorporating solar panels into the exterior walls of buildings. Both applications aim to harness solar energy while utilizing the underutilized space of buildings, contributing to sustainable energy generation and reducing the reliance on non-renewable energy sources.</p></p>
