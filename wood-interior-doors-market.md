<p><p>Wood interior doors can be categorized into two main types: hardwood doors and softwood doors. Hardwood doors are made from the wood of broad-leaved trees such as oak, mahogany, or walnut. They are known for their durability, strength, and natural beauty, making them a popular choice for high-end homes. Softwood doors, on the other hand, are made from the wood of coniferous trees like pine or fir. They are more affordable and widely available, but may require more maintenance and are generally less durable compared to hardwood doors.</p></p>
