<p><p>Mobile grain dryers are used for the drying of harvested grains and come in three types: mounted, self-propelled, and trailed. Mounted grain dryers are attached to a tractor or a truck and can be moved from one location to another. Self-propelled dryers are standalone machines with their own power source and wheels, enabling them to be easily maneuvered without the need for additional equipment. Trailed grain dryers are towed by a tractor and can be transported from one area to another. These types provide flexibility and convenience for farmers in drying their grains efficiently.</p></p>
