<p><p>The Desktop Roughness and Contour Measuring Machine market consists of two main types: Contact Roughness and Contour Measuring Machine, and Non-Contact Roughness and Contour Measuring Machine. Contact machines use probes or stylus to physically touch the surface, making direct measurements of roughness and contour. Non-contact machines, on the other hand, utilize optical or laser technology to scan the surface without physical contact, providing precise measurements without any surface damage. Both types cater to different needs and applications, allowing users to choose the most suitable method for their specific requirements.</p></p>
