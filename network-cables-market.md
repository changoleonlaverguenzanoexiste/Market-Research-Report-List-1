<p><p>The network cables market encompasses various types of cables used for different purposes. AS-i (Actuator Sensor Interface) cables enable communication between sensors and actuators in industrial automation systems. ATM (Asynchronous Transfer Mode) cables are designed for high-speed data transfer in telecommunications networks. CC-LINK (Control and Communication Link) cables facilitate communication between devices in industrial control systems. Fieldbus cables are used in industrial automation for communication between field devices and control systems. CANopen cables are specifically designed for connecting nodes in CANopen networks, commonly used in the automotive industry.</p></p>
