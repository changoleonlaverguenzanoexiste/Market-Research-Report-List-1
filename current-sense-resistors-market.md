<p><ul><li>SMD Type</li><li>Through Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Current sense resistors are used to measure current flowing through a circuit. The SMD-type market refers to surface mount technology, where the resistor is mounted directly onto the surface of the circuit board. This type offers smaller sizes, high precision, and is suitable for automated assembly. On the other hand, Through Hole type market refers to resistors that are inserted into holes on the circuit board and soldered on the other side. This type offers higher power handling capabilities, better mechanical strength, and is commonly used in high-current applications.</p></p>
