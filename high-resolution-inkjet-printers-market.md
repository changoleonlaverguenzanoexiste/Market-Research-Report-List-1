<p><p>High resolution inkjet printers are widely used in various industries for printing high-quality images and texts. There are two types of inkjet printers in the market. The first type is continuous inkjet printers, which use a continuous stream of ink droplets, some of which are deflected to create images or texts on the paper. The second type is drop-on-demand inkjet printers, where ink droplets are only generated when needed, resulting in higher precision and better control over the printed output. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on specific printing requirements.</p></p>
