<p><p>Complete denture teeth, also known as denture teeth or artificial teeth, are prosthetic teeth that are used to replace missing or lost teeth in individuals who require complete dentures. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing prevalence of dental disorders and tooth loss, the growing geriatric population, and advancements in dental technology. Additionally, the rising demand for aesthetic dentistry and the increasing awareness about the benefits of complete denture teeth among patients are driving market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing several trends that are expected to shape its growth trajectory. One such trend is the growing adoption of digital dentistry techniques in the fabrication of complete denture teeth. These techniques, which include computer-aided design/computer-aided manufacturing (CAD/CAM), allow for more precise and accurate customization of denture teeth, resulting in improved patient satisfaction.</p><p>Another trend is the increasing preference for metal-free denture teeth.
