These systems are simpler and more cost-effective, making them suitable for residential or small-scale commercial applications. On the other hand, Multiple Loops market involves the use of multiple control loops to manage different zones or areas within a building, providing more precise temperature control and flexibility for larger commercial or industrial spaces.</p></p>
