<p><p>Enteral tube feeding formula refers to specialized nutritional products used to provide nutrients to individuals who cannot consume food orally. There are three main types of enteral tube feeding formulas: polymeric, monomeric, and disease-specific formulas. Polymeric formulas contain intact proteins, carbohydrates, and fats in a balanced proportion, suitable for individuals with normal digestion and absorption. Monomeric formulas contain broken-down (hydrolyzed) proteins, carbohydrates, and fats, designed for individuals with compromised digestive function. Disease-specific formulas are tailored to meet the specific nutritional needs of individuals with certain medical conditions, such as diabetes or kidney disease.</p></p>
