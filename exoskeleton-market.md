<p><ul><li>Lower</li><li>Upper</li><li>Full Body</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The exoskeleton market is categorized into three types: lower body, upper body, and full body exoskeletons. Lower body exoskeletons primarily target individuals with lower limb disabilities, providing support and assistance for walking and regaining mobility. Upper body exoskeletons focus on aiding individuals with upper limb disabilities, enhancing their strength and dexterity for tasks involving the arms and hands. Full body exoskeletons encompass both lower and upper body assistance and are designed to provide comprehensive mobility and support for individuals with whole-body disabilities. These variations cater to different needs, enabling better accessibility and independence for people with various disabilities.</p></p>
