<p><strong>The Wet Electric Shaver Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ratory Shaver</li><li>Reciprocating Shaver</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wet electric shaver market comprises two main types: rotary shavers and reciprocating shavers. Rotary shavers are equipped with multiple circular blades that rotate in a circular motion. They are known for their ability to easily follow the contours of the face, ensuring a close and comfortable shave. On the other hand, reciprocating shavers feature straight blades that move back and forth in a linear motion. They are particularly effective for precision shaving and trimming of facial hair. Both types of shavers are designed to be used with water or shaving cream for a smoother and gentler shave.</p></p>
