<p><p>The high pressure relief valves market consists of various types of valves, including spring loaded pressure relief valves, pilot operated pressure relief valves, dead weight pressure relief valves, and others. Spring loaded pressure relief valves use a spring mechanism to relieve excess pressure in a system. Pilot operated pressure relief valves use a pilot valve to control the opening and closing of the main relief valve. Dead weight pressure relief valves rely on the weight of a disk or ball to relieve pressure. Other types of valves may include balanced bellows valves, pressure safety valves, or rupture discs.</p></p>
