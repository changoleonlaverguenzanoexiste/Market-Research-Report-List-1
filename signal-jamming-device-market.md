There are two types of signal jammers: specific signal jammers and multi-band channel signal jammers. Specific signal jammers target a single frequency or signal, effectively preventing communication within that range. On the other hand, multi-band channel signal jammers can disrupt multiple frequencies or signals simultaneously, making them more versatile and applicable in various situations. Both types of signal jammers are widely available in the market to cater to different needs and requirements.</p></p>
