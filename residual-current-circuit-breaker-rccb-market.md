<p><p>Residual Current Circuit Breakers (RCCBs) are categorized into different types based on their specific functionalities and applications. Type AC RCCBs are designed to detect AC earth fault currents. Type A RCCBs can detect both AC and pulsating DC earth fault currents. Type F RCCBs offer protection against fire hazards caused by faulty equipment. Type B RCCBs provide comprehensive protection against AC, pulsating DC, and smooth DC earth fault currents. There are also Other types of RCCBs available in the market catering to various specialized needs and requirements.</p></p>
