<p><ul><li>Helmholtz Type</li><li>Javal-Schiotz Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Corneal Curvature Instrument market consists of two primary types: Helmholtz Type and Javal-Schiotz Type. The Helmholtz Type utilizes an optical system to measure the curvature of the cornea, providing accurate and reliable results. On the other hand, the Javal-Schiotz Type employs the indentation principle to measure the corneal curvature, which can be less accurate but more cost-effective. Both types are essential tools in ophthalmology, helping to diagnose and treat various eye conditions related to the shape and curvature of the cornea.</p></p>
