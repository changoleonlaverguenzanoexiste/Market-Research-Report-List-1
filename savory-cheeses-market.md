<p><p>The savory cheeses market can be divided into two categories: shelf stable and refrigerated. These cheeses often undergo treatments such as pasteurization or aging to enhance their shelf life. On the other hand, refrigerated cheeses need to be stored at low temperatures to maintain their quality and prevent spoilage.
