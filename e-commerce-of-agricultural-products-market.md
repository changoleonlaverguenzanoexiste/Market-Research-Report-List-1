<p><p>E-commerce of agricultural products refers to the online buying and selling of various agricultural produce, such as fruits, vegetables, grains, dairy products, meat, and seafood. Factors driving this growth include the increasing adoption of smartphones and the internet, improving logistics and supply chain infrastructure, and the growing demand for fresh and organic produce. Firstly, there is a rising demand for locally sourced and sustainable produce, leading to the emergence of online platforms that connect farmers and consumers in the same geographic area.
