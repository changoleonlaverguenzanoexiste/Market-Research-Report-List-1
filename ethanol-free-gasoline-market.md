<p><p>Ethanol Free Gasoline refers to a type of fuel that does not contain any ethanol. It offers improved engine performance, particularly for older vehicles or outdoor power equipment such as lawnmowers, chainsaws, and boats, which may not be compatible with ethanol-blended fuels. One of the key factors driving this growth is the increasing demand from consumers who own classic or vintage vehicles that require ethanol-free fuel to avoid damage to engine components.
