<p><p>The shelling machine market consists of two main types: vertical type and horizontal type shelling machines. Vertical type shelling machines are designed with a vertical structure where the shell or husk of a product is removed using a downward force. These machines are often used for smaller products such as nuts or seeds. On the other hand, horizontal type shelling machines have a horizontal structure and use a rotating motion to strip off the shell or husk. These machines are commonly used for larger products like coconuts or fruits.</p></p>
