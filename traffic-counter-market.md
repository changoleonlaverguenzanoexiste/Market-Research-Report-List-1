<p><strong>The Traffic Counter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IR Beam</li><li>Thermal Imaging</li><li>Video Based</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The traffic counter market consists of various types, including IR Beam, Thermal Imaging, Video Based, and Others. IR Beam traffic counters utilize infrared beams to detect the presence of vehicles passing through the beam. Thermal Imaging counters use advanced thermal sensors to identify vehicles based on the heat they emit. Video Based counters employ cameras to capture video footage, which is then analyzed to count and categorize vehicles. Other types may include ultrasonic sensors, magnetic loops, or microwave radar. These different technologies provide options for monitoring and analyzing traffic flow in different environments and conditions.</p></p>
